Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sign-out?

A: On the profile tab there is a gear to the top right, tap that. The sign out option is at the bottom.

Q: How do I send a text on openup?

A: There is no texting on openup, you can voice call, video call and send audio messages. Ask for their number to text!

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: On the profile tab there is a gear to the top right, tap that. The delete account option is at the bottom.

Q: How do I report or block a user?

A: On the top right of any user profile are three dots, tap that and the options to block or report are there.

Q: Who is Tarlok?

A: He is the founder and your first friend of openup, if you have any issues let him know!
