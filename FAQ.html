Frequently Asked Questions

How do I not have my location be shown on the map?

On the map view, there is button with a map icon on the bottom middle right, tap that to disable your location or go into your iphone settings, search bonjour and disable location from there.

How do I sign-out or delete my account?

Tap your profile, the sign out and delete options are at the bottom.

How do I send a text on bonjour?

There is no texting on openup, you can send audio messages. Ask for their number to text!

How do I report or block a user?

A: On the bottom right of any user profile expanded are three dots, tap that and the options to block or report are there.
